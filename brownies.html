Chocolate Brownies

The best recipe for a super chocolatey, fudgy brownies

  1. Home
  2. Baking
  3. Brownies
  • Makes: 16
  • Cooking Time: 30-35 minutes
  • Oven Temperature: 170°C

Ingredients:

  • 185g unsalted butter
  • 185g dark chocolate
  • 85g plain flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 50g white chocolate chips
  • 50g milk chocolate chips
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g golden caster sugar
  • Optional: Sultanas and Oreo's

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 170°C and line a 20cm square tin with baking parchment.
  2. Add the butter and chocolate to a metal bowl and gently melt over a bain-marie. Once fully melted, remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly.
  3. Seive the plain flour and cocoa powder together into a small bowl.
  4. Break the eggs and add the sugar into an electic mixer and whisk until thick and creamy (3-8 minutes). The mixture is ready when it becomes pale and double its original volume.
  5. Pour the cooled chocolate mixture into the eggy mixture and gently fold together until fully combined.
  6. Add the flour mixture and fold in.
  7. Finally, fold in the chocolate chips and any additional fillings such as sultanas, oreos or raspberries.
  8. Pour the mixture into the tin and bake for 30-35 minutes (maybe slightly longer if you added additional fillings). The mixture is ready when the middle of the brownie doesn't wobble.
  9. Leave the brownie to fully cool in the tin before removing and cutting.
