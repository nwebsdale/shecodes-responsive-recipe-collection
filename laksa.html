Laksa

A spicy noodle soup popular in Southeast Asia

Laksa

A spicy noodle soup popular in Southeast Asia

  1. Home
  2. Cooking
  3. Laksa
  • Serves: 4
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • 3 spring onions, sliced and seperating white and green parts
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Blue Dragon red curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 chicken stock pot, dissolved in 800ml water
  • 2 Kaffir lime leaves
  • 2tbsp fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 3tbsp crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 lime
  • 6 chicken thighs, chopped
  • 4 egg noodle nests

Method:

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large saucepan. Add the whites of the spring onion, garlic and ginger. Fry for 1 minutes then add the curry paste and stir. After another minutes add the coconut milk and stir until smooth before adding the chicken stock. Bring to a simmer.
  2. Add the kaffir lime leaves, peanut butter and a pinch of sugar. Squeeze half the lime juice into the laksa. Simmer gently for 10 minutes and season to taste.
  3. Meanwhile, fry the chicken thighs in a seperate pan until cooked through. Set aside to rest.
  4. After the laksa has been cooking for 10 minutes, add the egg noodles and cook until soft (about 4 minutes). Add a splash of water if needed.
  5. Add the chicken.
  6. Serve in bowls and serve with the remaining lime, spring onion and coriander.
