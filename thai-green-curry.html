Thai Green Curry

Enjoy some Thai cusine with this healthy green curry

  1. Home
  2. Cooking
  3. Thai Green Curry
  • Serves: 4
  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • 6 shallots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g chicken thighs, chopped
  • 2tsp prepared lemongrass
  • 2tsp root ginger, grated
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 vegetable stock, dissolved in 150ml hot water
  • Blue Dragon thai green curry paste
  • 2tbsp thai fish sauce
  • 2tbsp fresh coriander

Method:

  1. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a deep frying pan. Add the shallots, garlic and chicken. Cook on a medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Add the lemongrass, ginger, coconut milk, chicken stock, curry paste, fish sauce and coriander. Simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked and the sauce has reduced a little.
  3. Serve with rice and/or nann bread.
