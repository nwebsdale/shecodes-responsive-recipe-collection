Lemon Drizzle Cake

This zesty cake is a classic tea-time staple

Lemon Drizzle Cake

This zesty cake is a classic tea-time staple

  1. Home
  2. Baking
  3. Lemon Drizzle Cake
  • Serves: 10
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Oven Temperature: 180°C

Ingredients:

  • 2 unwaxed lemons
  • 275g granulated sugar
  • 175g unsalted butter, softend, plus extra for greasing
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1.5tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Grease and line a 900g loaf tin.
  2. Finely zest the lemons.
  3. Add 175g of the sugar into a electic mixer with the butter, flour, baking powder, eggs and lemon zest. Mix until just combined and smooth.
  4. Pour the cake batter into the loaf tin and level the surface. Bake for 35 minutes or until golden and cooked through. Remove from the oven and allow to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before moving to a wire rack.
  5. Squeeze the lemons to get 3tbsp of lemon juice and mix this with the remaining 100g of sugar.
  6. Using a skewer, make 50 deep wholes in the top of the cake.
  7. Slowly spoon half the lemon juice mixture over the top of the cake. After 5 minutes spoon over the remain lemon juice mixture.
  8. Allow to cool fully before serving.
